Hi, I'm **Yifei Yuan (袁一飞)**, a Ph.D. Candidate in the **Biodynamics Lab** of the joint Biomedical Engineering program at the [New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT)](https://www.njit.edu/) and [Rutgers University](https://www.rutgers.edu/).

My research sits at the intersection of **reinforcement learning, robotics, and biomechanics**, with a focus on **rehabilitation exoskeletons**. I am especially interested in *learning control policies in simulation* and transferring them to real-world wearable robots that can assist and restore human movement.

I'm always happy to connect with people who share these interests — feel free to reach out at [yy72@njit.edu](mailto:yy72@njit.edu).
